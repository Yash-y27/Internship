Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

General Questions

1. What is HTML?

HTML stands for HyperText Markup Language. It is used to create web pages.

2. What is CSS?

CSS stands for Cascading Style Sheets. It is used to style web pages.

3. What is JavaScript?

JavaScript is a programming language used to make web pages interactive.

Account Questions

4. How do I create an account?

Click the Sign Up button and fill in the registration form.

5. How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions.

6. Can I change my email address?

Yes. Go to your account settings and update your email.

Orders & Payments

7. Which payment methods are accepted?

We accept UPI, credit cards, debit cards, and net banking.

8. Can I cancel my order?

Yes, you can cancel your order before it has been shipped.

9. How can I track my order?

You can track your order from the "My Orders" section.

Technical Support

10. Why is the website not loading?

Check your internet connection and refresh the page.

11. Which browsers are supported?

Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari are fully supported.

12. How do I contact support?

You can contact us using the Contact Us page or email support@example.com.
